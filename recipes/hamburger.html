Hamburger

Description

Classic American hamburger!

Ingredients

  • 4 oz 85/15 ground beef
  • salt
  • pepper
  • hamburger bun
  • shredded lettuce
  • tomato
  • ketchup
  • oil

Steps

  1. Slice tomato into slices
  2. Shape ground beef into shape of a patty and season both sides with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat pan to medium heat and toast both side of your hamburger bun.
  4. Heat pan to medium high heat and coat pan with a layer of oil.
  5. Cook patty and flip both sides frequently until the patty reaches an internal temperature of 165 degrees. Then remove from pan. Optionally you can add a slice of cheese.
  6. Add ketchup, and lettuce to the bottom of your bun. Then add your patty and a slice of tomato.
