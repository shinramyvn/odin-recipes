Ribeye Steak

Description

Perfectly cooked ribeye steak!

Ingredients

  • 12 oz ribeye steak
  • salt
  • pepper
  • garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 sprigs of rosemary
  • 1 tablespoon beef tallow

Steps

  1. Season the ribeye steak with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  2. Leave the steak out for at least two hours at room temperature.
  3. Heat pan to medium-high and add beef tallow when pan is hot.
  4. Cook steak and flip often until it reaches an internal temperature of 120 degrees.
  5. Turn heat of medium-low and add butter and rosemary.
  6. Use a spoon to baste the butter onto the steak until it reaches an internal temperature of 135 degrees. While flipping every 30 seconds.
  7. Rest steak on your cutting board for 10 minutes before slicing and enjoy!
