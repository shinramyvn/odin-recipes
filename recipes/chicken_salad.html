Chicken Salad

Description

Grilled chicken with fresh crisp lettuce, cherry tomatoes, cucumber and croutons. Add different topping and make it your own!

Ingredients

  • 1 head of romaine lettuce
  • 1 chicken thigh
  • croutons
  • cherry tomatoes
  • cucumber
  • dressing of your choice
  • salt
  • pepper
  • oil

Steps

  1. Cut romaine lettuce to small bite sized pieces.
  2. Wash all of your produce.
  3. Cut cherry tomatoes into halves and cut cucumber into slices.
  4. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
  5. Heat pan to medium high and add add oil when hot.
  6. Cook chicken for 7 minutes on each side or until it reaches an internal temperature of 165 degrees.
  7. Rest chicken on cutting board for 10 minutes.
  8. Cut chicken to slices.
  9. In a large mixing bowl, add lettuce, tomatoes, cucumber, chicken, croutons, and your dressing of choice.
  10. Mix well and serve.
